How we met

In a way even romantic comedy writers would roll their eyes at, Varsha and I met. In 2015, I was studying at SRM in Ramapuram, and she was a Dental student at the same university, but in Katankalathur. We met through a mutual friend at an event held on her campus. We didn't keep in touch thereafter, but as destiny would have it, we met once again in 2019. I was contemplating pursuing my Master's in Canada while working in Chennai. She was thinking about a Master's in dentistry in Chennai. We met one day and remained in touch thereafter. Texts became long calls, one thing led to another and we realized we were in love with each other. The time (August '19) arrived when I had to leave India for my master's. A lot of discussions later, we decided to stay together, yes, in a Long-distance relationship (and this wedding can vouch for it - we have completely bulldozed the LDR hoax). Since then, we’ve been in a long-distance relationship. I’m not going to lie, it was rocky at the start, and we found it tough for a few months. The time difference is tough for anyone, and it was a huge commitment. We decided to call and talk to each other every day no matter what. Days, months, years went by and she remains my favorite notification. 13,000 km apart and more than 2 years away, the time has come..
